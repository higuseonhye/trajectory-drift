# Human trajectory infrastructure

## Thesis

> Humans do not fail primarily from lack of intelligence.  
> They fail from **trajectory drift** under entropy, isolation, weak interaction loops, and broken momentum systems.

**trajectory-drift** is **organizational trajectory coherence infrastructure** — human + AI + teams under the same entropy.

---

## Core shift

| From | To |
|------|-----|
| AI observability | **Mission & coordination coherence** |
| Dashboards | **Trajectory stability surfaces** |
| Solo agent traces | **Human + AI propagation alignment** |
| What failed | **What drifts, what propagates, what recovers** |
