# Capital drift

Failure to understand ownership, leverage, investment, asset accumulation, and compounding behavior.

Not a critique of financial decisions — a diagnosis of **trajectory-level capital blindness**.

---

## Drift patterns

### Labor-only optimization

Optimizing hourly output, productivity metrics, and task completion without building assets, equity, or leverage.

**Signal:** High activity, flat ownership trajectory.

---

### Consumption masquerading as investment

Courses, tools, conferences, and networking framed as "investment" but producing no owned assets.

**Signal:** High spend (time/money) on inputs, low asset output.

---

### Asset avoidance

Discomfort with ownership, equity, capital, and investment concepts leading to dependency trajectories.

**Signal:** Moral drift overlap — capital topics avoided until constrained.

---

### Prestige capital

Spending on visibility, credentials, and status signals instead of compounding assets.

**Signal:** Prestige drift overlap — looking successful vs being positioned.

---

### Platform dependency

Building entirely on rented platforms without owned distribution, audience, or infrastructure.

**Signal:** Algorithm changes destroy trajectory overnight.

---

### AI labor acceleration

Using AI to do more labor faster without shifting toward ownership, systems, or leverage.

**Signal:** AI Drift overlap — speed without judgment compounding.
