# Drift taxonomy

Core categories of trajectory destruction — for humans, founders, operators, and organizations.

**Drift happens quietly.** Most people don't collapse — they fade: numbness, dead loops, lost curiosity, disconnection from reality — often in **dead environments** that suppress vitality.

Each entry is meant for **operational diagnosis**, not taxonomy for its own sake.

---

## Quiet drift (emotional layer)

Everyday drift — including while looking **functional**:

| Drift | Symptoms |
|-------|----------|
| **Inertia drift** | Moving through life without chosen direction |
| **Signal loss** | Can't tell what actually matters anymore |
| **Fake aliveness** | Productive, busy, dead inside |
| **Dead-system adaptation** | Optimizing inside a life never truly chosen |
| **Existential suppression** | "Fine" on surface, dishonest underneath |
| **Numbness / doomscroll** | Escape without signal |
| **Curiosity death** | Nothing surprises; no frontier pull |
| **Environmental drift** | Sterile spaces, sensory deprivation, awe loss, indoor survival loops |
| **State collapse** | Functional externally, numb/alive-deprived internally |

**Not drift:** unconventional paths, messy transformation, external instability toward truth.

**Recovery:** not "return to normal" — reconnect with genuine signal, restore aliveness, one intentional turn (including **environmental turns**). See [trajectory-native/docs/steering.md](https://github.com/higuseonhye/return/blob/main/docs/steering.md) · [environment-design](https://github.com/higuseonhye/return/blob/main/docs/environment-design.md).

## Environmental drift (structural)

**Definition:** Trajectory loss through lifeless surroundings, sensory flattening, and atmospheres that suppress vitality — while remaining functional.

**Symptoms:** Prolonged fluorescent/indoor loops; scroll-feed dominance; no nature or scale contact; emotionally sterile routines; adaptation to dead systems; high output, low aliveness.

**Examples:** Fluorescent office existence · fake networking spaces · endless scrolling environments · extractive productivity culture · disconnected urban survival loops.

**Recovery:** Environmental turns — natural light, nature, movement, aesthetic coherence, meaningful conversation, spaces that restore awe. See [trajectory-native/framework/environment-design/](https://github.com/higuseonhye/return/tree/main/framework/environment-design).

## Human trajectory drift

### Prestige Drift

**Definition:** Optimizing for looking impressive instead of building ownership and value.

**Symptoms:** Status signaling, credential accumulation, performative building, visibility without assets.

**Recovery:** [`../recovery/`](../recovery/) — restore ownership focus, reduce prestige loops.

---

### Apprentice Drift

**Definition:** Infinite learning without ownership, distribution, assets, or execution.

**Symptoms:** Endless courses, tutorials, certifications; no shipped work; consumption masquerading as progress.

**Recovery:** Force application milestones; build assets; close learning loops in reality.

---

### Labor Drift

**Definition:** Becoming an increasingly optimized worker without leverage or capital.

**Symptoms:** High productivity, no assets; time-for-money optimization; AI accelerates labor without ownership.

**Recovery:** [`../capital-drift/`](../capital-drift/) — shift toward asset building and leverage.

---

### Noise Drift

**Definition:** Losing cognitive bandwidth to irrelevant trends, feeds, reactions, and urgency.

**Symptoms:** Constant context switching; reactive behavior; trend-chasing; feed addiction.

**Recovery:** Reduce noise inputs; restore long-range focus; protect deep work blocks.

---

### Moral Drift

**Definition:** Avoiding money, capital, and investment awareness until life becomes constrained and dependent.

**Symptoms:** Discomfort with capital topics; consumption over investment; delayed ownership building.

**Recovery:** Capital literacy (not investment advice); ownership mindset; [`../capital-drift/`](../capital-drift/).

---

### Spiritual Drift

**Definition:** Losing purpose, integrity, direction, or deeper alignment due to speed, status, or desire.

**Symptoms:** Values-action divergence; burnout; emptiness despite achievement; integrity compromises.

**Recovery:** Coherence restoration; [`../../trajectory-native/framework/coherence/`](https://github.com/higuseonhye/return/tree/main/framework/coherence).

---

### Organizational Drift

**Definition:** Teams and systems lose mission coherence, alignment, and institutional memory.

**Symptoms:** Execution continues; strategy quietly shifts; repeated incidents; meeting entropy.

**Recovery:** [`../organizational-drift/`](../organizational-drift/) · [`../recovery/`](../recovery/).

---

### AI Drift

**Definition:** Tool hype and automation theater replacing judgment, ownership, and trajectory coherence.

**Symptoms:** AI for speed alone; agent orchestration without decision memory; productivity aesthetics; no compounding.

**Recovery:** Judgment infrastructure; decision memory; ownership layer — see product mapping.
